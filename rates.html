Studio Policies 

The following terms and conditions apply to all bookings at Bay Pilates & Wellness.
♣ Sessions and classes are by appointment only.
♣ We happily accept block studio session bookings at the same day / time each week. 
♣ Classes are scheduled on a “first come, first served” basis.
♣ Pre-registration and prepayment are required.

♣ An additional 2.75% will be charged for all credit card transactions.

All cancellations made less than 24 hours prior to lesson will be charged the full rate of that lesson.
